About Housing Price Toolkit



When selling and buying houses, one is often very interested in knowing the historical and future trends of housing prices in the particular location of interest. This toolset is designed for these purposes.

Zillow Home Value Index (ZHVI) is used because it provides a fair assessment of price for a given time, as it accounts for changing composition of properties sold in one time period versus another time.

Prediction of housing prices and its evaluation are made. The historical prices data are divided into training set (2/3 of data) and cross-validation set (1/3 of data). Linear regression is performed on the training set to model price one year into the future using prices in the past one year. Then the proposed model is evaluated using the cross-valiation set, and the mean error is reported.




Data are provided by Zillow Inc .
